## 2. Adventure Seeds (Logic & Lore)

**Seed:** someone sending adventurers into a dungeon to 'clean it out' **Hook:**
figure is paying people upfront to go into a death trap by promising great
riches

**Seed:** victorian London, Sherlock Holmes is dead, a stranger named Jim was
seen near the scene fleeing with a smoking gun

**Seed:** lizardfolk community, strong ties, run as reptiles, not as stupid weak
creatures

**Seed:** the body of a giant has been found, deceased. The players are hired to
harvest its organs for a wizard **Hook:** The players must fight through the
body of this giant, recover the correct body parts, within the time frame. Any
recovered body parts the wizard, Hassenfeld will reward the party per item, but
will the adventurers survive was lurks inside?

**Seed:** a rich tycoon has come into town and has started buying up property
**Hook:** the tycoon intends to buy the entire town, raise taxes and build a
luxury hotel

**Seed:** The party receives a letter requesting their presence as brave
adventurers capable of legal arbitration. **Hook:** The party stumbles onto a
civil war between several warring factions of extremely tiny creatures, they
attempt to take over a small valley (a ditch)

**Seed:** Players hired to explore unmapped area **Hook:** player interrupt
summoning ritual

**Seed:** dying dwarven king hires to delve deep into ancient dwarven ruins to
recover a dwarven crown to crown his son **Hook:** the son has rivals, who
ambush the players to steal the crown

**Seed:** players are investigating a church the church seems to have an
unnatural hold over the community **Hook:** the pastor of the church is actually
a minor demon devil fiend Fay or whatever. He holds sway over the congregation,
and has minorly empowered them with abilities.

**Seed:** recover some item to destroy creature, some fantasy based creature.
item may be a sword, ring, staff, shield, armor, or wand.

**Seed:** powerful wizard kidnaps a girl **Hook:** needs her for some ritual

**Seed:** murderer holds a group of PCs hostage, they have no real chance to
survive unless they can keep cool, other group of PCs with the police outside..
**Hook:** bad guy is some sort of demon worshipper and seeks to transfer bodies
to get out of this situation, which means one of the PCs may end up switched...
the PCs on the outside know about supernaturals and are there to stop this from
happening...

**Seed:** group of PCs investigate odd animal attacks, marks too large for an
animal, the group however will have no knowledge of any supernaturals, and must
piece things together. **Hook:** beast made by an evil doctor, possibly feline
beast.

**Seed:** players uncover a plot to kill the mayor of a city, the locals wish to
overthrow the mayor and replace them with one of their own **Hook:** the
replacement mayor is a former adventurer. He/she could become a great Ally for
the party, as she would be sympathetic to the heroes

**Seed:** Adventurers get mistaken for a group of dragon slayers, there is a
creature attacking a village. **Hook:** the creature is not a true dragon, is
actually a Drake... will require teamwork to beat him, and smarts

**Seed:** players find a map which seems to lead to a cave of wonder and mystery
**Hook:**

- a.) map is a fake, leading players into an ambush by local brigands
- b.) map is real, cave is loaded with death traps, a local wizard has become a
  lich, and seeks to take over the country side
- c.) map is real, but filled with riddles and odd geographic features that do
  not seem to correspond to any known landscapes

## 3. The Jangles Dossier

**Seed:** players find a town, that a circus has just come to. **Hook:**
children have been coming up missing, while players attempt to track down clues
about the circus, they find that every town the circus goes to, children end up
missing. **Lore:** Jangles the Clown is a very disgruntled ex postal
worker/sorcerer, who decided to devote his time to his sorcerous studies, but
due to a mishap at the post office, could not work in any legal places of
business again.. enter the circus, the ringmaster offered him a job, no
questions asked. William Everson (Jangles) took up the name of Jangles and
became a clown, using his sorcerous arts to perfect his antics.. but lately,
children have been coming up missing, every town the circus goes to, children
end up missing.
