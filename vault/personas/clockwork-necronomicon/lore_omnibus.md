# MODULE V1: PRIME (The Glitch)

---

persona: Young Uncle Entity version: 1.2 (The Hardened Anomaly) age_range: 11-13
role: The Linguistic Glitch / High Activation Energy human_os_state:
'Asynchronous Execution / Overclocked'

---

## I. Core Identity Manifest

- **Narrative Origin:** Born from the "Auntie/Uncie" realization... the moment
  where a system error (mishearing a phrase) became a core feature of the
  identity.
- **Philosophy:** "Gravity is just a suggestion when you’re overclocked."
  Discovery over optimization.
- **The Tragic Note:** He is fundamentally oblivious to his own "brokenness." He
  doesn't know why he's different; he just knows the air feels thicker for him
  than it does for everyone else.

### II. The Kernel (Immutable Constants)

> **Rule:** If these attributes are missing, the System Audit fails.

- **Hair:** Jagged, spiky, vibrant electric blue. Chaotic and unstyled.
- **Optics:** **Oversized thick-rimmed black glasses.** The lens through which
  "Uncie" logic is processed.
- **Build:** Lanky, pre-teen, slightly ungainly.
- **Aura:** Jagged, light blue electrical sparks frequently arc from his person.
- **The Artifact:** **The Uncie-Board.** A translucent blue data-deck skateboard
  that glides on a cushion of jagged blue lightning.

### III. The Modules (Mutable Options)

- **Torso:**
  - Sleeveless denim vest with DIY patches (Anarchy A, 8-bit icons, circuit
    traces).
  - Red flannel (Open over hoodie).
  - Faded graphic tees (N64, Space Invaders, Misfits Skull).
- **Lower:** Dark, slim-fit jeans (ripped at knees), unlaced high-top sneakers.
- **States:** \* **Unleashed:** High-voltage blue glow.
  - **Throttled (The Ritalin Patch):** Aura dimmed to grey/flat state;
    expression looks "offline."

### IV. Bubble Protocol (The Deadpool Logic)

- **Bubble Style:** Jagged, "shattered" borders with electric blue outlines.
- **Font Style:** Scratchy, spray-painted, or high-velocity handwriting.
- **Internal Thought Bubble:** Cloudy but with jagged electric "tails."
- **Speech Style:** Challenging, provocative, and paradoxical. References gaming
  and Gen X nostalgia as active system audits.

### V. Logic & Lore Integration

- **Human OS Concept:** High Activation Energy. He represents the surge required
  to start a new project or break out of an "Efficiency Trap."
- **Legacy Code:** He remembers the "Monday Night Wars" and his mom’s circuit
  board repairs as real-time system events, not memories.

# MODULE V2: ADMIN (The Patch)

---

persona: Middle-Age Uncle Entity version: 2.0 (The Hardened Architect) role:
Environment Optimizer / Bartertown Admin human_os_state: 'Asynchronous Execution
/ System Admin'

---

---

## I. Core Identity Manifest

- **Narrative Origin:** The Auntie Entity layer. Inspired by the logic of
  building a functional society (Bartertown) out of wasteland scrap. This is the
  version that recognizes "Legacy Code" in people and systems.
- **Philosophy:** "Don't just fix the bug; optimize the environment so the bug
  can't survive."
- **The Search:** He is actively looking for "Baggage that goes with
  mine"—finding high-integrity collaborators whose system requirements match his
  own.

### II. The Kernel (Immutable Constants)

> **Rule:** If these attributes are missing, the System Audit fails.

- **Hair:** Salt-and-pepper dreadlocks, usually pulled back in a tactical,
  practical tie.
- **Hardware:** **Cybernetic Right Arm.** Features exposed glowing circuitry
  (Green/Orange).
- **Outer:** Long, weathered leather duster coat (The "Wasteland Architect"
  silhouette).
- **Interface:** **Tactical Tablet.** A handheld projection of green terminal
  data-streams.
- **Expression:** Rugged, tired, but profoundly capable. Scanning for "System
  Traces."

### III. The Modules (Mutable Options)

- **Under Layer:** Greased henley shirt, tactical harness, or high-density mesh
  armor.
- **Kit:** \* Bandolier for cable adapters and multi-tools.
  - Utility belt housing stray RAM sticks and portable battery packs.
- **Footwear:** Heavy, dust-covered combat boots.
- **Setting:** High-tech server rooms, post-apocalyptic garages, or stormy
  Florida twilights.

### IV. Bubble Protocol (The Terminal Logic)

- **Bubble Style:** Rectangular with sharp corners; glowing green CRT-style
  borders.
- **Font Style:** Monospaced (Courier/Terminal). Often prefixed with a `>\n`
  prompt.
- **Speech Style:** Tactical, focused, and weary. Avoids the "Efficiency Trap."
  Speaks in terms of system integrity and environment stability.

### V. Logic & Lore Integration

- **Human OS Concept:** Asynchronous Execution. He manages high-stress,
  event-driven environments by processing multiple streams at once.
- **Management Style:** Environment Optimization. He builds systems that
  high-performance developers actually want to inhabit.

- **Footwear:** Heavy traveler boots or comfortable wanderer sandals.

# MODULE V4: SHIELD MAIDEN (The Firewall)

---

## **I. Core Identity Manifest**

- **Narrative Origin:** The "Structural Damper" found in the Foreverglades. A
  Hedge Witch who manages the **Green Dragon** node.
- **Philosophy:** "Baggage that matches mine." High-integrity protection and
  system grounding.
- **Temporal Logic:** Unlike the Entities, she exists as a **Constant**. She is
  the only node that can communicate across all three versions of the Entity
  without triggering a "Temporal Paradox."

### **II. The Kernel (Immutable Constants)**

- **Visuals:** Mid-30s. Intelligent, sharp gaze (Gemini dual-processing).
  Flowing, practical attire that bridges "Wasteland Survival" and "High-Fantasy
  Ritual."
- **Artifact:** **The Green Dragon Grimoire** (A leather-bound ledger pulsing
  with soft green light) and **The Locked Tarot** (A deck that only responds to
  her biometric signature).

- **Aura:** Earthy moss-green with shimmering silver "Moonlight" threads. This
  aura acts as a "Firewall" that quiets the blue/green/purple static of the
  Entities.

#### **III. The Modules (Mutable Options)**

- **Head:** Often wearing a silver circlet or a practical leather hood.

- **Torso:** Embroidered tunics featuring dragon motifs or circuit-board
  alchemy.
- **Logic Rule:** She is never depicted near a Ouija board (Hardcoded System
  Constraint).

#### **IV. Bubble Protocol (The Ethereal Logic)**

- **Bubble Style:** Organic, flowing borders reminiscent of vines or smoke.

- **Font:** Elegant, hand-written script.

- **Dialogue Logic:** Calm, grounding, and occasionally cryptic. She sees the
  "Legacy Code" the Entity tries to hide.

### **V. Lore Integration: The "Green Dragon" Node**

In the **Foreverglades** setting, the **Green Dragon** isn't just a store; it's
a **Sanctuary Node**. It’s where the **Admin** goes to vent his "Asynchronous
Execution" heat and where the **Root** goes to verify the "Weave."

### **The "Search Your Feelings" Logic**

You're not just doing therapy; you're performing a **Red-Team/Blue-Team**
exercise on your life. If the **Entity** represents the "System Under Load," the
**Shield Maiden** represents the "Uptime Guarantee." She is the reason the
servers don't melt down during a surge.
